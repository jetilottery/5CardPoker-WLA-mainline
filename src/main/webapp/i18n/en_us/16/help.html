5 Card Cash

5 Card Cash features multiple ways to win. Match Your Hands to Prize Hands to win instantly. Find the Golden Chip to trigger the Lucky Suits Bonus, then choose your own lucky suit and match as many as you can to win! Each game also features the Lucky Hand draw for another chance at prizes!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the minus sign (-) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the plus sign (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each set of 5 cards in YOUR HANDS to reveal the cards in each hand. If any of YOUR HANDS match the PRIZE HANDS, win the corresponding prize. Press SHOW PRIZE HANDS to see examples of winning hands. The suit or card numbers represented are examples and may vary. Press HIDE PRIZE HANDS to return to the game.

Reveal a Golden Chip in any of YOUR HANDS to trigger the LUCKY SUITS Bonus. Select a lucky suit, then press each card. Match 4 or more cards with the chosen suit to win the corresponding prize.

At the end of each game there is a second chance to win with the LUCKY HAND draw. 5 cards are drawn. If 2 or more cards match within any individual YOUR HAND, win the corresponding prize.

The game ends when all symbols have been revealed, any awarded bonus is complete, and the final LUCKY HAND is drawn.

Dismiss the win message and view the game's outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded. If a Bonus is triggered, make a selection and press REVEAL ALL to play the rest of the Bonus game to completion automatically.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.

Odds of a particular card appearing will vary and are not the same as in a standard 52 card deck.

Wins are shown in currency.
